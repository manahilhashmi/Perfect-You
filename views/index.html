Your Eating Scores

Take the test and assess your eating scores today!

Your scores are relative to other people who face similar eating challenge. The results describe to you where you stand and allows you to assess yourself. It is free of cost and gives a quick and personal consultation.
The scores should not be taken as final diagnosis because they are only an amalgamation of eating questionnaires administered worldwide.

"I struggled, I fought, I survived..."

I did not know that I was facing a challenge. I did not know that they name my condition. Everyone just thought that I am dieting. My parents used to scold me. I had no friends left because all of them thought that I have gone crazy. I wanted to stop but I could not.

One day my mother took me to a family doctor. That's when my life began to change. He discussed my eating routines with me. He assured me that eating won't make me fat.
